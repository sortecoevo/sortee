### Board of Directors

This is an elected committee that is ultimately responsible for SORTEE.
Three positions on the board come open each year and are elected by the
members of SORTEE in November. These terms last for three (3) years.

 
 

**Members serving from 2026 through 2028 are:**

![Ineta Kačergytė](Ineta_Kačergytė.jpg)  
Ineta Kačergytė (she/her)
([Website](https://www.slu.se/en/profilepages/k/ineta-kacergyte/)) is a
postdoctoral researcher at the Swedish University of Agricultural
Sciences, where Ineta evaluates conservation efforts to restore wetlands
for biodiversity.

 

![Aidan Tan](Aidan_Tan.png)  
Aidan Tan
([Website](https://www.sydney.edu.au/medicine-health/about/our-people/academic-staff/aidan-tan.html))
is a paediatric doctor, governance leader and long-time advocate for
open, reliable and transparent science who cares deeply about
strengthening SORTEE's global community through principled, inclusive
and accountable research culture.

 

![Juliette Tariel-Adam](J_Tariel-Adam.png)
